§ 308106.
Updates and improvements to Battlefield Reports
Not later than 2 years after the date of the enactment of this section, and every 10 years thereafter, the Secretary shall submit to Congress a report that updates the Battlefield Reports to reflect—
(1)
preservation activities carried out at the battlefields in the period since the publication of the most recent Battlefield Reports update;
(2)
changes in the condition, including core and study areas, of the battlefields during that period; and
(3)
any other relevant developments relating to the battlefields during that period.
