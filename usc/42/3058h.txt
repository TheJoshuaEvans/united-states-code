§ 3058h.
Regulations
The Assistant Secretary shall issue and periodically update regulations respecting—
(1)
conflicts of interest by persons described in subparagraphs (A) and (B) of section 3058g(f)(1) of this title; and
(2)
the relationships described in clauses (i) through (vi) of section 3058g(f)(1)(C) of this title.
