§ 2006.
Authorization of appropriations
There are authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary to carry out this chapter, to remain available until expended—
(1)
$15,000,000 for fiscal year 2006;
(2)
$20,000,000 for fiscal year 2007;
(3)
$30,000,000 for fiscal year 2008;
(4)
$40,000,000 for fiscal year 2009; and
(5)
$50,000,000 for fiscal year 2010.
