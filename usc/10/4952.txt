§ 4952.
Purposes
The purposes of the program authorized by this chapter are—
(1)
to increase assistance by the Department of Defense to eligible entities furnishing procurement technical assistance to business entities;
(2)
to assist eligible entities in the payment of the costs of establishing and carrying out new procurement technical assistance programs and maintaining existing procurement technical assistance programs; and
(3)
to foster innovation for the defense industrial base and to diversify and expand the defense industrial base.
