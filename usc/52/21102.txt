§ 21102.
Process for adoption
The adoption of the voluntary guidance under this part shall be carried out by the Commission in a manner that provides for each of the following:
(1)
Publication of notice of the proposed recommendations in the Federal Register.
(2)
An opportunity for public comment on the proposed recommendations.
(3)
An opportunity for a public hearing on the record.
(4)
Publication of the final recommendations in the Federal Register.
