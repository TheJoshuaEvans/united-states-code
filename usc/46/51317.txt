§ 51317.
Adjunct professors
(a)
In General.—
The Maritime Administrator may establish a program for the purpose of contracting with individuals as personal services contractors to provide services as adjunct professors at the Academy, if the Maritime Administrator determines that there is a need for adjunct professors and the need is not of permanent duration.
(b)
Contract Requirements.—
Each contract under the program—
(1)
must be approved by the Maritime Administrator;
(2)
shall be for a duration, including options, of not to exceed one year unless the Maritime Administrator finds that exceptional circumstances justify an extension of up to one additional year; and
(3)
shall be subject to the availability of appropriations.
