§ 51908.
Prohibition against unauthorized manufacture, sale, possession, or display of awards
(a)
Prohibition.—
Except as authorized by this chapter or the Secretary of Transportation, a person may not manufacture, sell, possess, or display a decoration or medal provided for in this chapter.
(b)
Civil Penalty.—
A person violating this section is liable to the United States Government for a civil penalty of not more than $2,000.
