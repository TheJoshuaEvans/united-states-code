§ 13110.
Authority of Comptroller General
(a)
Access to Financial Disclosure Reports.—
The Comptroller General shall have access to financial disclosure reports filed under this subchapter for the purposes of carrying out the Comptroller General’s statutory responsibilities.
(b)
Studies.—
Not later than December 31, 1992, and regularly thereafter, the Comptroller General shall conduct a study to determine whether the provisions of this subchapter are being carried out effectively.
