§ 20926.
Period for implementation by jurisdictions
(a)
Deadline
Each jurisdiction shall implement this subchapter before the later of—
(1)
3 years after July 27, 2006; and
(2)
1 year after the date on which the software described in section 20925 of this title is available.
(b)
Extensions
The Attorney General may authorize up to two 1-year extensions of the deadline.
