§ 21307.
Allocation of increased formula grant funds
The Attorney General shall allocate an increase in the amount provided to a State under the covered formula grants under this chapter such that—
(1)
25 percent the amount of the increase is provided under the program described in section 21301(1)(A) of this title; and
(2)
75 percent 1 the amount of the increase is provided under the program described in section 21301(1)(B) of this title.
