§ 70909.
Relationship to Federal agencies
(a)
In General.—
On request of the board of directors, the FFA may collaborate with Federal agencies, including the Department of Education and the Department of Agriculture on matters of mutual interest and benefit.
(b)
Agency Assistance.—
Those Federal agencies may make personnel, services, and facilities available to administer or assist in the administration of the activities of the FFA.
(c)
Agency Compensation.—
Personnel of the Federal agencies may not receive compensation from the FFA for their services, except that travel and other legitimate expenses as defined by the Federal agencies and approved by the board may be paid.
(d)
Cooperation With State Boards.—
The Federal agencies also may cooperate with State boards and other organizations for career and technical education to assist in the promotion of activities of the FFA.
