§ 220502.
Organization
(a)
Federal Charter.—
The corporation is a federally chartered corporation.
(b)
Perpetual Existence.—
Except as otherwise provided, the corporation has perpetual existence.
(c)
References to United States Olympic Association and United States Olympic Committee.—
Any reference to the United States Olympic Association or the United States Olympic Committee is deemed to refer to the United States Olympic and Paralympic Committee.
