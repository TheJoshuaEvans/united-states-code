§ 30102.
Purposes
The purposes of the corporation are—
(1)
to assist individuals throughout the United States in solving their social and economic problems and in their health and educational and character development;
(2)
to promote the use, by other lay and professional agencies and workers, of the techniques of that assistance developed by the corporation; and
(3)
to receive, invest, and disburse funds and hold property for the purposes of the corporation.
