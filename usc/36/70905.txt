§ 70905.
National officers
(a)
Composition.—
There shall be not less than 6 national student officers of the FFA, including a student president, 4 student vice presidents (each representing regions as provided in the constitution or bylaws of the corporation), and a student secretary.
(b)
Election.—
The national officers of the corporation shall be elected annually by a majority vote of the delegates assembled in the annual national convention from among qualified members of the corporation.
(c)
Vote at National Convention.—
Each qualified delegate has one vote at the annual national convention.
